Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
1. What payment methods do you support?
 We support multiple payment methods, including major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express) as well as Alipay.
2. Can I use Alipay?
 Yes. If you are using our services in China, you can purchase one-time services with Alipay.
3. How are credits deducted for image and video generation?
 Our pricing for image and video generation is aligned with the official channels. If bulk purchases from the official channels come with discounts, we will also pass those discounts on to you.

4. Do my credits expire?
 Our credit expiration policy is very flexible. Credits will only expire 12 calendar months after they are acquired, giving you plenty of time to use them.
5. What if I need more credits?
 You can earn free credits by publishing content daily.
 In addition, you can get more credits by subscribing to a membership or purchasing credits directly.
6. How can I check my credit status?
 Simply click on your credit balance to view detailed changes and usage history.
7. Are there any hidden fees?
 No. The price you see is the price you pay. There are no hidden charges beyond the plan you select.
