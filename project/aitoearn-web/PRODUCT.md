# Product Context: AiToEarn Web

## Register

product

## Purpose

AiToEarn is an AI-assisted content growth and monetization workspace for creators, marketers, advertisers, operators, and creator teams. The product turns AI-assisted content creation into an end-to-end workflow: create, adapt, schedule, publish, and track content across multiple platforms with minimal repeated setup.

Its core object is the Content Agent: a configurable AI creator that supports the full loop from content creation to platform adaptation, multi-account publishing, analytics, and monetization.

## Users

- Creators and creator teams managing multi-platform publishing workflows.
- Brand and advertiser operators managing campaigns, tasks, and account interactions.
- Growth operators who need efficient content production, account binding, publishing, analytics, and monetization workflows.
- Marketers and operations teams who need reliable high-volume social content workflows.

## Product Principles

- Keep complex publishing flows explicit, predictable, and recoverable.
- Keep task workflows calm, dense, and trustworthy; the interface should disappear into the work.
- Preserve familiar product UI conventions for forms, dialogs, tabs, media controls, and navigation.
- Use the brand purple-to-cyan gradient only for explicit action, state, progress, identity, and page seam roles.
- Keep work surfaces neutral, readable, and fully compatible with light and dark themes.
- Reuse shared components, tokens, and route conventions before adding new UI primitives.
- Optimize for fast decision-making in dense task surfaces.

## Brand Personality

Professional, calm, efficient, modern, capable, and creator-friendly. The experience should feel like a reliable growth studio for high-volume content operations while still carrying AiToEarn's AI growth and monetization energy.

## Anti-References

- Over-decorated work surfaces that distract from publishing, analysis, or campaign tasks.
- Inconsistent dialogs, forms, tabs, navigation affordances, or platform-specific flows that feel stitched together.
- Hard-to-read low-contrast controls or muted text on tinted surfaces.
- Full-bleed gradient backgrounds, gradient text, or ornamental brand effects that do not communicate action or state.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use semantic controls, visible focus states, readable contrast, keyboard-operable dialogs and media controls, and motion that communicates state rather than decoration. Design should remain clear in light and dark themes.
